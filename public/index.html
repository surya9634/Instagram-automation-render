🤖 Instagram Business DM Automation

Automate your Instagram Business direct messages based on comment triggers. Connect via Facebook to access your Instagram Business account.

Required Permissions:

  • 📊 Access to your Instagram Business account
  • 💬 Read comments on your posts
  • 📨 Send automated direct messages
  • 📱 View your Instagram media
  • 👥 Access to connected Facebook Page

How it works:

  1. Connect your Instagram Business account via Facebook
  2. Select posts you want to monitor for comments
  3. Add trigger words (hotwords) for each post
  4. Set automated DM responses for each trigger
  5. We automatically send DMs when users comment your hotwords
